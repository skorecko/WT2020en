Pine

Basic Data

Pine is a softwood coniferous tree.

Latin name:
Pinus
Division:
Pinophyta
Class:
Pinopsida

Where You Can Find Pines

Pines grow in

  1. Europe and
  2. Asia.

My Opinion

I like pines, because

  • they have long needles,
  • smell nice and
  • thanks to the softwood they are easy to cut.
